The categorization of various paranormal subjects, grouped by relative danger to the populace or nature.

## Ranks
### Rank 4
Relatively harmless and non anomalous. Subjects which should be monitored but do not require termination or containment.
### Rank 3
Dangerous and should be treated with caution and/or fairly abnormal. Subjects should be sought after and contained if possible. If containment is not possible termination will be considered.
### Rank 2
Very dangerous and should be treated with extreme caution and/or shows extreme signs of abnormal, paranormal, or dangerous levels of meta-normality. Containment is recommended, if containment is not possible termination is required. If termination is not possible resources must be exhausted to prevent significant damage to populace.
### Rank 1
Subjects showing danger of a fundamental or foundational quality (eg. causing extreme instability in the fabric of reality or its destruction). Subjects must be terminated whenever possible, if termination is not possible, subject must be contained. Should a subject show high enough meta-normality Omega Protocol "Forget-Me-Not" should be enacted immediately.
