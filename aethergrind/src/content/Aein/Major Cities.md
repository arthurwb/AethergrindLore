There are 4 major cities in [[Aein]]: *[[Clover Station]]*, *Barpool*, *Se'ban*, and *Hatcher*.

These are not just "large" cities, these are *MEGA cities*. City states which encompass multiple cities within them.

*Clover Station* is a hive city centered over a huge pit and commands all *western trade*.

*Barpool* is the *largest of all the cities* and takes up most of the northern coast of Suveria.

*Se'ban* is the center for most *metallurgy and eastern trade*.

*Hatcher* is the *technological heart* of the world, producing most of the technology that the world now uses. It has been completely leveled multiple times.
