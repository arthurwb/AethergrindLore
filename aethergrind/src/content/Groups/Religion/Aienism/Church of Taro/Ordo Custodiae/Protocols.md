## Alpha
**Alpha Protocol "Righteous Fist"**: Ordo Pugnae is contacted and a strike force made up of at least 30% Ordo Custodiae personnel is formed with the remainder personnel to be made up of Pugnae personnel. Pugnae personnel are to remain under command of Ordo Pugnae though they are to be supervised by Custodiae personnel.
## Omega
**Omega Protocol "Forget-Me-Not"**: All data held by Ordo Custodiae, Ordo Doctrinae, and Ordo Pugnae is to be backed up using *Alistair's Loop* and immediately stored in extra dimensional containment space 1.001.
