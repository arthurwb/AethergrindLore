Linear Polygonal Freehand Runes

A popular form of freehand runes which relies on the use of geometric symbols to represent spells. As with all freehand runic systems the grammar is very limited, but because of the ease of use many scribes use freehand runes like Castov Freehand as a quick way to apply runic magic.

Castov freehand can only be used when a caster is present and actively observing the rune and its effects. The use comes in allowing rune scribes to effectivly cast spells without needing to undergo the training that a wizard would, allowing them to apply their knowledge of rune crafting into creating a more immediate effects.
